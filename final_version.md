My name is Morgan Swann. I currently work in home improvement, but my background and passion are rooted in the beverage alcohol industry, where I have over five years of experience across both on- and off-premise environments. I began my hospitality career in 2014 and earned my WSET Level 2 certification in wine in 2024. Wine, spirits, and beer are both my professional expertise and something I genuinely love.

## The Problem

The beverage alcohol industry is experiencing a significant challenge. Many consumers feel overwhelmed by the number of available products and, lacking the knowledge to choose confidently, are opting out. Alcohol consumption has declined sharply, from 67% in 2022 to 54% in 2025, due to health concerns and limited product education among shoppers. The industry risks losing not only sales, but also entire generations of potential consumers who never found their entry point.[^1]

[^1]: Ansira, "2026 Alcohol Trends: What to Expect in the Coming Year," January 2026, https://ansira.com/blog/2026-alcohol-trends/

## How AI Could Help

- **Personalized product discovery and recommendations** tiered across good, better, and best options — meeting consumers at their budget and taste preferences without requiring them to already know what they like
- **Food pairing suggestions and cocktail/mocktail recipes** tailored to what a consumer is already buying or browsing, turning a single bottle into an experience
- **Education on ratings and awards** — demystifying scores, medals, and critical acclaim so consumers can shop with confidence rather than confusion
